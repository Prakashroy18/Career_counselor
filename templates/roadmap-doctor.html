High School (12th Grade)

  • Science stream with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB).
  • Prepare for NEET-UG (medical entrance exam).

Undergraduate (MBBS)

  • 5.5 years including internship.
  • Admission through NEET-UG.

Postgraduate Specialization (MD/MS)

  • 3 years after MBBS.
  • Admission via NEET-PG.
  • Specialize: Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, etc.

Super-specialization (DM/MCh)

  • Another 3 years after PG.
  • Super-specialize: Cardiology, Neurology, etc.

Career/Jobs

  • Medical practitioner, surgeon, researcher, government/army medical services.
