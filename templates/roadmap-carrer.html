High School (10th SSC)

  • Focus on matriculation exams, fundamentals.
  • Explore streams: Science, Commerce, Arts.

Intermediate (MPC, Biotech, Commerce)

  • MPC: Prepares for engineering entrances like JEE, EAMCET.
  • Biotech: Leads toward medical or life sciences.
  • Commerce: Business and finance fundamentals.

Engineering (BTech)

  • Multiple specializations available.
  • Focus on projects, internships, certifications.

Post Graduate (MTech, MBA, MSc)

  • Advanced specialization and research.
  • Prepare for GATE, NET, other relevant exams.

Software Development Career

  • Learn programming, data structures, algorithms.
  • Gain experience in full-stack, DevOps, cloud.

Data Science Career

  • Master statistics, machine learning, AI.
  • Work on real datasets and projects.

Management Career

  • Develop leadership, team building skills.
  • Pursue MBA or equivalent to accelerate growth.

Entrepreneurship Path

  • Build ideas into products and services.
  • Learn business planning, funding, marketing.
